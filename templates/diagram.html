Project Diagrams

Explore the architectural blueprints of DriveSafe through our comprehensive diagrams. These visuals detail the system's structure, interactions, and data relationships, providing a deeper understanding of its functionality.

Flow Diagram (ERD)

The ERD illustrates the database schema, highlighting entities such as users, trips, alerts, and locations, and their relationships. This design ensures efficient data management and retrieval for the app's core features.

Sequence Diagrams

Sequence diagrams detail the interactions between different components of the system, such as the user interface, backend services, and external APIs. These diagrams clarify the flow of operations during critical processes like trip initiation, wrong-way detection, and alert delivery.

Class Diagrams

Class diagrams outline the structure of the software, defining classes, their attributes, and methods. This view helps understand the organization of the codebase and the responsibilities of different software modules within the DriveSafe application.

Project Overview

SafeRoute is a student-led initiative focused on developing a robust system for detecting and alerting wrong-way drivers on highways. Our solution leverages computer vision and machine learning to analyze real-time video feeds from traffic cameras, identifying vehicles traveling against the flow of traffic. Upon detection, the system triggers immediate alerts to traffic management centers and nearby drivers, minimizing the risk of collisions.
